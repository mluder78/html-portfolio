The Best Movies According to Mandy

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Almost Famous

I love this movie because I like Kate Hudson's character Penny. She has a very fun, free spirited aura bout her. It is set in the 1970's where a young reporter follows a band and their groupies. It is a fun movie to watch.

Dazed and Confused

This is a fun movie chronicaling the last day of school for Teenagers and the party that they go to afterwards.

The Breakfast Club

This movie is about some highschool students that have gotten themselves into trouble. They are stuck on a Saturday in detention. They end up learning that they might have more incommon than they realize.
